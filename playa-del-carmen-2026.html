🎉 Things To Do
More than just the beach (but also... the beach)
🏊

Cenotes

Crystal-clear natural swimming holes hidden in the jungle. Magical for kids and adults alike.

🐢

Akumal Bay

Snorkel with sea turtles right from the beach. A bucket-list experience for the whole family.

🏛️

Tulum Ruins

Ancient Mayan ruins overlooking the Caribbean. History meets breathtaking views.

🎢

Xcaret Park

50+ attractions: underground rivers, butterfly gardens, jaguar island, and a spectacular night show.

🐒

Punta Laguna

Spot spider monkeys in the wild, go canoeing, and try zip-lining through the canopy.

🛍️

5th Avenue

Playa's famous pedestrian street lined with shops, restaurants, and street performers.

🐊

Crococun Zoo

Get up close with monkeys, macaws, lizards, and more on a guided jungle tour.

🕳️

Río Secreto

Explore stunning underground caves and cenotes with helmets and headlamps. Truly unforgettable.
